To access project work rooms AD111 student workshop, and AD113 soldering and test room, as well as some EN building facilities, students must first do the online modules. After completion of the modules students must then be inducted into the actual rooms, either as part of the enrolled group for group projects/practical classes or by one of the technical staff for individual or small group projects. Once inducted the student's ID card is then activated for door access to the rooms.

Some project work activities will require a risk assessment form to be completed, for example (but not limited to) projects that involve the use of Lithium Polymer (LiPo) batteries, high voltage, lasers, flammable chemicals and gases, carbon monoxide, bacteria etc. Contact technical staff or

Raffaele Timpano, Safety Coordinator, People & Culture, room AGSE340, phone (9214) 4954, mail H11, email rtimpano@swin.edu.au

for any enquiries regarding the risk assessment procedure.
