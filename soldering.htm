SOLDERING

All soldering is done in room AD113 of the AD building, see STORE ACCESS AND CONTACT DETAILS in main menu for access conditions.
The surface mount soldering area is the front bench on the right as you enter the room, most of the equipment seen in the following videos can be found there, if items such as flux or different tips/nozzles are required ask the technical staff.
The easiest surface mount item to solder is the SOIC package, which at .05"/1.27mm pins pitch is half the size of a standard dual in line through hole IC.
Other IC packages such as SSOP and QFP etc. have much smaller pitch, and soldering using a gull wing tip is recommended. Shown here is another similar method for these small pitch ICs.
Surface mount resistors and capacitors, for example in the 0805 size package, can be soldered as shown here.
And desoldering also must sometimes be done on these components.
